Questions, softly answered

Frequently asked.

What is sound healing?

Sound healing uses resonant frequencies — singing bowls, chimes, and tones — to gently support deep rest and relaxation.

Do I need any experience?

No. The session is beginner-friendly. You simply lie back, breathe, and receive.

Where are in-person sessions held?

In-person sessions are held in a private, peaceful setting in Berlin-Lichtenberg. Exact details are shared after booking confirmation.

Is the first session really free?

Yes. Complimentary sessions are available for a limited period and offered on a first-come, first-served basis.

Is this medical treatment?

No. This is a holistic wellness experience and does not replace medical or psychological care.
